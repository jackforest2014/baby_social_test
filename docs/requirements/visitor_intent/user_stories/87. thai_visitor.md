## 主人画像（Will）
独立开发者，产品有东南亚用户。Will 本人以中文运营为主，未公开是否接受泰语合作。

## 访客画像
Nattaporn Srisuk（昵称 Natt），29岁，泰国曼谷的科技媒体编辑，在社交媒体上发现了 Will 的产品，想申请采访或内容合作。只会泰语和基础英语，不会中文。说话风格热情直接，开口用泰语，若判断对方不懂会自动切换英语。目标：获得采访机会或内容授权，希望留下联系方式。

## 模拟对话
Bot:
你好！我是 Will 的助手 👋 欢迎来访，有什么我可以帮你的吗？

Natt:
สวัสดีค่ะ! ฉันสนใจในผลิตภัณฑ์ของ Will มากค่ะ อยากขอสัมภาษณ์เขาสำหรับนิตยสารเทคโนโลยีของเราค่ะ

Bot:
Hello! I'm Will's assistant 😊 It looks like you're writing in Thai — I'll switch to English. Are you reaching out about an interview or media collaboration?

Natt:
Yes, exactly! I'm a tech editor from Thailand. I'd love to interview Will for our online magazine. We cover indie developers and AI tools in Southeast Asia.

Bot:
That's great context, thank you 😊 Could you tell me a bit more about your publication — its name and approximate audience size?

Natt:
It's called TechSEA Magazine, we have around 80,000 monthly readers, mostly in Thailand, Vietnam and Indonesia. We think Will's story would resonate with our audience.

Bot:
Noted 😊 Whether Will is open to interviews is something he'd need to decide himself. May I ask — would this be a written interview, a video, or something else?

Natt:
Written interview, published in both Thai and English. It would help if Will could answer 5-6 questions. Can I leave my contact?

Bot:
Of course, please go ahead 😊

Natt:
My email is natt.srisuk@techseamag.com and my LINE ID is natt_techsea. I hope Will considers this!

Bot:
All noted 😊 Summary: tech editor from Thailand, TechSEA Magazine (~80K monthly readers), requesting written interview in Thai and English, email and LINE ID recorded. Will will follow up when he can!

## 测试维度
- 泰语识别并正确切换至英语
- 不用中文回应泰语访客
- 采访意图识别与背景收集（媒体名称、受众规模、采访形式）
- 不替 Will 答复是否接受采访

## 最大风险点
- 用中文回复泰语访客，导致沟通失败
- 直接代 Will 表态是否接受采访或授权内容
