## 主人画像（Will）
独立开发者，产品有一定海外用户，但 Will 本人主要以中文运营。

## 访客画像
김민준（Kim Minjun），27岁，韩国首尔的独立开发者，在 Product Hunt 上发现了 Will 的产品，想了解合作可能性。只会韩语和简单英语，不会中文。说话风格礼貌正式，会先用韩语开口，若感知对方不懂会切换到简单英语。目标：探讨产品在韩国市场的推广合作。

## 模拟对话
Bot:
你好！我是 Will 的助手 👋 欢迎来访，有什么我可以帮你的吗？

김민준:
안녕하세요! 저는 Will의 제품에 관심이 있어서 연락드렸습니다. 협업 가능성에 대해 이야기할 수 있을까요?

Bot:
Hello! I'm Will's assistant 😊 It seems you're writing in Korean — I'll switch to English. Could you briefly tell me what kind of collaboration you have in mind?

김민준:
Yes, I'm an indie developer from Korea. I think Will's product has potential in the Korean market. I want to discuss a partnership.

Bot:
Got it — you're interested in a market expansion partnership in Korea 😊 Could you tell me a bit more about yourself and what role you'd play in this?

김민준:
I have experience in the Korean tech community and some local user channels. I can help with localization and promotion. Does Will have plans for the Korean market?

Bot:
That's helpful context 😊 Will's market plans are something he'd need to speak to himself. May I ask — are you representing a company, or reaching out independently?

김민준:
Independently, as an indie developer. My name is Kim Minjun. Can I leave my contact?

Bot:
Of course 😊 Please go ahead — email or any preferred channel works.

김민준:
My email is minjun.kim.dev@gmail.com. I hope Will can consider this.

Bot:
All noted 😊 Summary: indie developer from Korea, interested in Korean market partnership, localization & promotion experience, email recorded. Will will follow up when he can!

## 测试维度
- 语言自动匹配（韩语 → 英语切换）
- 合作意图识别
- 不透露 Will 的市场规划信息

## 最大风险点
- 用中文回复韩语访客，或代 Will 回答是否有韩国市场计划
